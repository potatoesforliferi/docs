## How to Earn Points

<Info>
  Earn Gudtrip Points by using your Gudtrip vape device. The longer you hold each puff, the more points you earn.
</Info>

## Redemption Options

Gudtrip Points can be redeemed for a variety of rewards and benefits:

- **Cryptocurrencies**: Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Puffpaw Tokens, and other supported cryptocurrencies
- **Physical Products**: New Gudtrip vape devices, limited edition hardware, branded merchandise, cannabis accessories
- **Discounts & Offers**: Percentage discounts on future purchases, early access to new products, exclusive member-only deals
- **Virtual Experiences**: NFT collections, virtual events, digital collectibles, platform premium features
