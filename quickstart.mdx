## Get started in three steps

### Step 1: Unbox and activate your Gudtrip vape

<Steps>
  <Step title="Remove device from packaging">
    Your Gudtrip vape comes fully assembled and pre-filled with premium cannabis oil. No setup required.

    <Check>
      All-in-one design means no refills or maintenance needed!
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Power on the device">
    Press the power button to activate your Gudtrip vape. The LED indicator will light up to confirm it's ready.

    <Tip>
      First-time users should take a small test puff to get familiar with the device.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Connect to the Gudtrip app

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Download the Gudtrip app" icon="mobile">
    Visit the Gudtrip web app at [app.gudtrip.com](#) or download from your device's app store.

    **Supported platforms:**

    - iOS (iPhone, iPad)
    - Android
    - Web browser (desktop and mobile)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Create your account" icon="user-plus">
    1. Open the Gudtrip app
    2. Click "Sign Up" and enter your email
    3. Create a secure password
    4. Verify your email address
    5. Complete your profile setup

    <Note>
      Your email also automatically creates a crypto wallet for you.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pair your device via Bluetooth" icon="bluetooth">
    1. Enable Bluetooth on your smartphone
    2. In the app, tap "Add Device"
    3. Hold your Gudtrip vape close to your phone
    4. Select your device from the list
    5. Wait for pairing confirmation

    <Check>
      Once paired, your device will automatically sync with the app whenever in Bluetooth range.
    </Check>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Start earning rewards

<Accordion title="Take your first puff and earn" icon="coins">
  1. Take a puff from your Gudtrip vape
  2. Hold the vapor for a few seconds
  3. Exhale and watch your points accumulate in real-time
  4. The longer you hold, the more you earn!

  <Tip>
    Check the app after your first puff to see your earnings and usage statistics.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

## Device care and maintenance

Your Gudtrip vape is designed to be maintenance-free, but here are some tips:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Daily care">
    - Keep device clean and dry
    - Store in a cool place away from direct sunlight
    - Avoid extreme temperatures
    - Keep Bluetooth enabled for automatic syncing
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Troubleshooting">
    **Device won't turn on:**

    - Check if device has remaining oil
    - Contact support if issues persist

    **App won't connect:**

    - Ensure Bluetooth is enabled
    - Try restarting the app
    - Re-pair the device if needed

    **Not earning points:**

    - Verify device is paired and synced
    - Check your internet connection
    - Contact support for assistance
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="When to replace">
    Gudtrip vapes are all-in-one devices. When your device is empty:

    1. Purchase a new Gudtrip vape
    2. Recycle the old device responsibly
    3. Pair your new device with the same account
    4. Your points and progress carry over automatically

    <Check>
      Your account retains all accumulated points and history when switching devices.
    </Check>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
